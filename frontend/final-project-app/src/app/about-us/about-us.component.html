AUGUR Metric Visualisation Version 2.3

About:

The purpose of our site is to display real-time data from Augur in various graph formats.

Specifically, we aimed for you to be able to visualise the different repositories and groups in Augur.

On our site, you should be able to compare the pushes/pulls, additions, issues and contributions of various Augur groups.
